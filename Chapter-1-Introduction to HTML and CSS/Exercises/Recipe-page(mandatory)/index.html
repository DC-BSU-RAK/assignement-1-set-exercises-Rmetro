Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara stands as an Italian classic pasta dish formed by combination of eggs alongside cheese and pancetta and pepper. This pasta dish stands out due to its creamy delicious taste and it offers simple preparation steps.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, small dice
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated aged Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Add salt and black pepper to your taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Prepare the spaghetti by boiling salted water until it reaches an al dente texture. Drain afterwards.
  2. Whisk together a bowl that contains eggs with Parmesan cheese and black pepper.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a pan and cook the pancetta until crispy.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and mix in the drained pasta.
  6. Quickly add the egg mixture and stir well, allowing the heat from the pasta to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top.
