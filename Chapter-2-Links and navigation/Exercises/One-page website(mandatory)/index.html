EAFC 25

Overview

EAFC 25 is the EA Sports FC franchise latest installment, featuring enhanced gameplay, realistic graphics and various new features.

Publisher: Electronic Arts
Platforms: PS5, Xbox Series X/S, PC
Release Date: TBD 2025

Real life players, teams and leagues of the Premier League, La Liga and more, feature in EAFC 25.

Reviews
